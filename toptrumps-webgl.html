    How to play 🎴

    1. Pick the stat on your card you think will beat the CPU's hidden card. Click a row, or press 1–6.
    2. Highest wins the round and takes both cards — except for Price (▼), where lowest wins.
    3. Ties send both cards to the pot. Next round's winner sweeps the pot too.
    4. Empty the CPU's deck to win the match. Each card is a real Azure VM with real specs.

    💡 Tap the ⓘ next to a card's family (e.g. "General Purpose") to learn what that VM family is for.
